FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Medlorix?

Medlorix is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to help you diagnose symptoms, find nearby clinics, book appointments, and access health-related information all from one place.

How does the symptom checker work?

Simply input your symptoms into our symptom checker, and our AI will analyze them to suggest possible diagnoses. Please remember, this is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

How can I find a nearby clinic?

Use our clinic search feature to select your country, state, local government, and nearest bus stop. Our platform will show you a list of nearby clinics with details, reviews, and availability.

How do I book an appointment?

Once you find a clinic, you can view their availability and book an appointment directly through our platform. You will receive a confirmation and reminder of your appointment via your email address.

What if I need emergency guidance?

Describe your emergency situation in our emergency guidance tool. Our AI will provide first aid steps and immediate actions you can take while waiting for professional help.

Can I read health and nutrition articles on Medlorix?

Yes, we feature a range of health and nutrition articles. You can browse and read them directly on our platform to stay informed and healthy.

How do I sign up as a mental health expert?

Currently, we are working on expanding our mental health support services. If you're interested in joining our network of experts, please check back soon for more details.

Is my data secure with Medlorix?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security, implementing strong encryption and following stringent data protection practices.

How can I contact Medlorix for support?

For any questions or support needs, you can reach us via the contact form on our website, or email us at support@medlorix.com.

When will new features be available?

We're continuously working on improving our platform. Keep an eye on our updates and announcements for information on new features and enhancements.
